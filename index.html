ENROLLMENT PROCESS

  • You chat with us to share your concerns, or you call us directly on our toll-free numbers to know how we can help you.
  • You attend our free-trial classes
  • Once you are satisfied and sure that you want to engage with us, you fill the consent form.
  • You pay for the services.
  • Based on your requirement, we assign a tutor to you or ask you to choose from the list of tutors available.
  • We set up your account and grant you access to an online classroom.
  • You contact your tutor and start enjoying interactive sessions.

PRICING

There is no fixed price for the courses as each course is customized to suit the needs of an individual. The price depends on the complexity of the subject, level of attention required, and mode of learning you choose. But you need not worry as we would work with you to offer you the best and that too within your budget. We also have a flexible payment policy whereby you can pay us upfront or in installments. You can pay us in cash or pay online using payment gateways.
